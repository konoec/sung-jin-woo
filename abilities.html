Abilities

Sung Jin-Woo possesses a unique power granted by the System, allowing him to continuously grow stronger without limits. His abilities include:

  1. The System
    • A game-like interface that enables him to level up, receive quests, and unlock new skills.
    • Grants stat points, inventory space, and the ability to evolve.
  2. Shadow Extraction & Shadow Army
    • Sung Jin-Woo can resurrect fallen enemies as loyal shadow soldiers.
    • He commands a vast Shadow Army, including high-level creatures like Igris, Beru, and Bellion.
  3. Dagger Mastery
    • Expert in dual-wielding daggers, using speed and agility to overwhelm enemies.
    • Can strike with incredible precision and power.
  4. High-Speed Regeneration
    • His body heals rapidly, making him nearly immortal in battle.
    • Wounds that would be fatal for others heal in seconds.
  5. Stealth & Assassination Techniques
    • Can turn invisible using the Stealth skill.
    • Uses surprise attacks and overwhelming speed to eliminate enemies before they react.
  6. Ruler’s Authority
    • A telekinetic ability allowing him to manipulate objects and attack from a distance.
    • Can lift, crush, or repel enemies using pure force.
  7. Monarch’s Power
    • As the Shadow Monarch, he can rival gods and other Monarchs in battle.
    • His mere presence instills fear in enemies, and he can erase entire armies alone.
